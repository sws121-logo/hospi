Our Story

Established in 2005, SmileBright Dental Hospital has been providing comprehensive dental care with a focus on patient comfort and advanced treatment methodologies.

Our facility features the latest in dental technology, including digital X-rays, 3D imaging, and laser dentistry, ensuring precise diagnoses and effective treatments.

Modern Dental Facility

Our Journey

2005 - Foundation

SmileBright Dental Hospital was established with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality dental care.

2010 - Expansion

We expanded our facility and introduced advanced dental technology including digital X-rays.

2015 - Dental Lab

Our in-house dental laboratory was established to provide custom dental prosthetics.

2020 - Digital Dentistry

We implemented CAD/CAM technology and 3D printing for precise dental restorations.

2023 - Today

Continuing to innovate with laser dentistry and minimally invasive techniques.
